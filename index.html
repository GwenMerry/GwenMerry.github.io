Who We Are

Studley Community Choir is a vibrant musical ensemble founded by Nat, a music enthusiast dedicated to fostering a love for singing and the numerous health benefits it brings. Our choir is driven by the vision of creating an inclusive and affordable space where people from all walks of life can come together, connect, and unite through the power of song. Nat's passion for making music accessible to as many individuals as possible led to the establishment of this welcoming community. Guided by the expertise of our musical director, Abbie, and the musical curation of Gwen, we offer a diverse repertoire spanning various genres.

Why Sing?

Singing is good for the soul, and we 100% agree with this! Singing regularly is proven to reduce stress and help with confidence building. Not only does singing work those throat muscles but singing with a choir is a great way of making new friends. Our aim is to be completely inclusive and welcoming, it doesn't matter if you've never tried singing before or if you are used to performing. We aim to be a warm environment where you can have a go and boost your self esteem whilst having good fun. There is no need to be able to read music, but if you can, great! We work together to achieve a wonderful sound which we are proud to share with people! Give us a try!

Meet the team

Nat

The passionate owner of the choir, founded the ensemble driven by her deep love for music and the joy of singing in a communal setting. Recognizing the numerous health benefits associated with group singing, she envisioned a choir that would be inclusive and accessible to a diverse range of individuals. Nat aimed to create a safe and affordable space where people could connect through song and a shared purpose. Fueled by a desire to remove barriers and offer a repertoire spanning various musical genres, Nat encountered challenges in finding local choirs that embraced these values. Undeterred, she took matters into her own hands and successfully launched the Studley Community Choir..

Abbie

The choirs conductor, has worked with many musical groups over her 30 years of working in music. Trained as a classical pianist with a degree in music, Abbie has been a Musical Director in the theatre and teaches voice and other instruments both privately and in schools. Abbie has worked for Yamaha Music Foundation, Italia Conti, Stagecoach Theatre Arts and various theatres. Abbie believes that if you can speak you can sing! Singing is a joy, especially in a choir and she chooses a variety of music to capture this joy

Gwen

Our admin person. Gwen sources all of our music and lyrics and has also created this website. Gwen also helps with musical arrangements.Gwen is also a member of the choir, a talented musician who plays many instruments and sings in various choral groups.

Want to Join?

Joining Studley Community Choir is simple and welcoming. Interested individuals can become part of the musical community by attending the choir sessions held every Wednesday evening from 6:30 to 8:30 at Studley Methodist Church. The doors are open to all, and no prior experience or auditions are required; just bring your love for singing and a desire to connect through music. Alternatively, for those who prefer a more direct approach, reaching out to the choir can be done through their social media channels. Whether it's a message, comment, or direct inquiry, the choir encourages prospective members to connect with them through their social media platforms, making the joining process accessible and accommodating for everyone. Studley Community Choir eagerly awaits new members to join in the joy of singing and community bonding.
